OpenText is transitioning to AI-Powered SAST as the default approach for analyzing COBOL code, replacing traditional translator-based engines that struggle with the language's many dialects. The AI method eliminates per-dialect parsers, covers all COBOL versions, supports incremental scanning, and reduces false positives through contextual analysis while keeping results consistent across scans.
